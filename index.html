Berkeley Tax Measure Impact Calculator

How much will Berkeley's proposed tax measures cost you — and what happens if some don't pass?

Berkeley property tax measures come in two flavors. Some are charged based on your home's assessed value (AV) — the higher your AV, the more you pay. Others are charged per square foot (sq ft) of your home — the bigger your home, the more you pay. Measure names below are colored to match: blue = AV-based, orange = sq ft-based. Even if no new measures pass, your bill will still go up every year: under Proposition 13, California limits AV growth to 2% per year, and Berkeley's assessed values increase by that full 2% annually. That baseline increase is already built into the result below. Additionally, there are new sales taxes also being proposed.
